Itinerary — 6 Days / 5 Nights

Day 1 — Athens to Patmos

Meet 14h00 at the port. Evening ferry (18h00) to Patmos — your boutique journey begins.

Day 2 — Patmos

Breakfast, guided tour of the Cave of the Apocalypse & Monastery; windmills; dinner.

Day 3 — Island Leisure

Breakfast, self-guided exploring & beach time; dinner. Late ferry back to Athens.

Day 4 — Athens

Early arrival, breakfast, pottery class; food & backstreet tour; dinner & local wine.

Day 5 — Corinth

Guided trip to ancient Corinth; return in afternoon; celebration dinner.

Day 6 — Farewell

Early breakfast and transfers to the airport — memories that last a lifetime.
